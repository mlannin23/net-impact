Consulting Practice

Net Impact Berkeley is a student run, non-profit organization that provides professional consulting services to for-profits, non-profits and social enterprises. Each semester, we draw from our expertise in market research, growth and scalability, financial modeling, competitive analysis, marketing and environmental analysis to provide our clients with comprehensive and practical solutions.
