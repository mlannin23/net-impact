Mission

Net Impact Berkeley Undergrad is a non-profit, student-run consulting group on the UC Berkeley campus. Our undergraduate chapter is unique for being the premier student group that consults for social enterprises, non-profits and sustainable businesses. We provide services to our clients, including impact and financial analyses, marketing, research, and corporate social responsibility.

We believe in the power of first-hand experience working with leading industry professionals to create social or environmental impact. Each semester, project teams, consisting of four to five associates and led by a project manager, work together in close relationship with our clients.

Our clients are changemakers with an interest in social and environmental sustainability; and we strive to use our business expertise to help them make a “net impact” in their industry. For a detailed description of the services we offer, please click here.

Core Values

Responsibility, Innovation, Collaboration

Purpose

We bring responsibility to businesses and innovation to responsibility, empowering individuals to drive social and environmental change.

Vision

We strive to create a net impact in this world so that outdated practices are changed and lives are transformed in a way that embraces social responsibility and environmental stewardship.

Net Impact Central

Net Impact is an international organization of over 30,000 members at the undergraduate, graduate and professional level, who use their passion for business to tackle the world’s toughest problems. We use our skills for good, showing the world that it’s possible to benefit not just the bottom line, but people and the planet too. Net Impact Berkeley’s mission is aligned with that of its parent organization, using consulting to further the mission of corporate responsibility for our clients. Read more about our parent organization, Net Impact Central.
