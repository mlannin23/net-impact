Real Impact, Sustainable Results

Net Impact Berkeley is an undergraduate consulting organization dedicated to providing real businesses with lasting results. We draw from our diverse backgrounds to analyze complex business problems, develop meaningful insights and provide actionable results to improve our clients’ business practice. From Fortune 500 companies to non-profits, Net Impact Berkeley provides creative yet practical solutions that visibly improve our clients’ bottoms line, social responsibility, and environmental sustainability.

To see how we've improved our clients' practice, and our community, take a look at some of our published reports and UC Berkeley 2013 Sustainability Report (coming soon).
